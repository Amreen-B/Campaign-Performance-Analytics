# Business Recommendations Report
## HSBC Banking Campaign Analytics — June 2025

---

## Executive Summary

Analysis of 50,000 campaign contacts across 5 campaigns, 4 channels, and 5 customer segments reveals an overall ROI of **397%** and a conversion rate of **0.39%**. Seven evidence-based recommendations are outlined below, collectively projected to increase annual conversions by **35–40%** and revenue by **INR 45,000–60,000**.

---

## Finding 1 · Campaign Portfolio — Scale Winners, Fix Losers

| Campaign | ROI | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Personal Loan | 957% | Scale +30% budget |
| Wealth Advisory | 763% | Scale +20% budget |
| Credit Card Upgrade | 90% | Maintain, A/B test offers |
| Insurance Cross-Sell | 23% | Reduce spend, redesign offer |
| Savings Account | −59% | Pause and overhaul |

**Recommendation:** Reallocate 20% of Savings Account budget to Personal Loan and Wealth Advisory immediately.

---

## Finding 2 · Segment Strategy — Prioritise Premier and Mass Affluent

| Segment | Conv Rate | Rev/Customer |
|---|---|---|
| Mass Affluent | 0.74% | INR 9.51 |
| Premier | 0.63% | INR 4.09 |
| Retail | 0.28% | INR 2.19 |
| Student | 0.15% | INR 0.90 |
| Salary | 0.28% | INR 0.89 |

**Recommendation:** Increase Premier + Mass Affluent targeting by 25%. Student and Salary segments require redesigned offers with lower-ticket products before scaling.

## Finding 3 · Channel Reallocation — Email and SMS Are Most Efficient

| Channel | ROI | Cost Per Acq |
|---|---|---|
| Email | 1,325% | INR 66 |
| SMS | 932% | INR 64 |
| Mobile App | 573% | INR 90 |
| Call Center | 37% | INR 465 |

**Recommendation:** Shift 10% of Call Center volume to Email for mass campaigns. Reserve Call Center for Mass Affluent and Premier segments only (where the high-touch approach justifies the cost).

---

## Finding 4 · A/B Testing — Extend Duration Before Deciding

Current test duration produces no statistically significant results (all p > 0.05). Sample sizes are sufficient; the lack of significance suggests variants are genuinely similar.

**Recommendation:** Run a redesigned Variant B with a materially different offer (not just copy changes) for a further 60 days. Target a minimum detectable effect of +0.1 percentage points at 80% power.

---

## Finding 5 · Conversion Funnel — Fix the Open-Stage Drop-Off

| Stage | Count | Drop-off |
|---|---|---|
| Sent | 50,000 | — |
| Opened | 14,473 | **71%** ← biggest leak |
| Clicked | 5,469 | 62% |
| Responded | 3,156 | 42% |
| Converted | 196 | 94% |

**Recommendation:** A/B test subject lines and SMS sender names to improve open rate from 29% to 35% (achievable based on industry benchmarks). Each percentage point gained at this stage generates approximately 15 additional conversions.

---

## Finding 6 · CLV Targeting Gap

Mass Affluent has the highest average CLV but represents only 10% of campaign volume. Champions and Loyal tiers in RFM analysis are under-served with premium offers.

**Recommendation:** Create a dedicated "Priority" campaign track for the top 20% CLV customers (RFM score ≥ 11) with personalised offers, call-centre outreach, and relationship manager involvement.

---

## Finding 7 · Cohort Insight — Engage Mid-Tenure Customers

Customers with 3–7 year tenure (cohort years 2018–2022) show the highest revenue per contact. Newer customers (cohort 2024–2025) have low conversion because they haven't established product relationships yet.

**Recommendation:** Design an "onboarding journey" campaign for customers in their first 12 months to accelerate their progression to Loyal and Champion RFM tiers.
